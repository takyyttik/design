Diving into a website redesign

Based in Hawaii and California, ohwhale.co is home to creative and stylized goodies. A mix of original and kpop inspired fan merchandise to produce stickers, pins, keychains, and more.

Stepping away from a button heavy site, my rendition is a one-stop shop for ohwhale.co consumers, old and new, to shop and learn more about what’s coming up.

In the final design, there are three pages which include a landing, about, and an announcements/upcoming information page. Graphics are derived from original artwork, instagram posts, and product shots created by Kai, the mastermind behind ohwhale.co, to enhance the look and feel of the website to match the aesthetic of the brand.
