Frau Feuer

One "Miss Fire" that always hits

The name Frau Feuer is a play on words; directly translated from German, “Frau” is how one may refer to a woman “Miss,” while “Feuer” is the word for “Fire.” When put together it becomes “Miss Fire,” a character standing as a figure of guidance, like a coach.
